# The Rise of Agentic Solutions

Agentic AI is no longer a futuristic concept. It's the **backbone** of modern business operations.

## Why Agents?
Agents don't just "talk"; they *act*. They can use tools, navigate web interfaces, and make decisions within a set of constraints.

### The Agentic Stack
- **Cognition**: LLMs like Gemini 1.5 Pro or DeepSeek.
- **Action**: MCP tools and custom scripts.
- **Memory**: Persistent context and SQLite databases.
